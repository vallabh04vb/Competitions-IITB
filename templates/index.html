About Competitions

The Competitions at Abhyuday, IIT Bombay serve as a platform to cultivate sustainable solutions for societal challenges by fostering engagement with communities and stakeholders. Geared towards inspiring the youth to address social issues through innovation, these competitions have reached over 25 states and 50 cities, drawing participation from more than 1000 teams and involving 2500+ students and professionals nationwide.
