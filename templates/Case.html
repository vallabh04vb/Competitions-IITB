Description:

Welcome to the CASE STUDY COMPI - where intellect meets innovation, and problem-solving takes center stage. Our competition is a thrilling journey into the heart of real-world challenges, a platform for showcasing your creativity and analytical prowess, and an opportunity to make your mark in the world of critical thinking.

Structure:

  • Round 1: Preliminary:
    • Participants will submit an abstract containing a brief solution/research to the problem statement/sector study.
    • The abstract file should be 1-2 slides long.
    • Deadline for Registration: 25th December, 2023 11:59 PM
  • Round 2: Pre-Finals:
    • Participants will craft a detailed and concise presentation of max 12-15 slides.
    • The presentation should address the problem statement or the provided case study.
    • Submission Deadline: 17 Jan 2023, Wed 11:59 PM
  • Final Round: Finals:
    • Participants will prepare a 15-minute presentation on their Round 2 submission.
    • They will deliver this presentation during the second round of the competition in an offline setting.
    • Following their presentation, there will be a brief 4 to 5-minute Q&A session with the panel of judges.

    • Rules & Regulations:

  • Each team should consist of a maximum of 1-3 members.
  • The naming convention for abstract submission should be Leadername_teamname.
  • All decisions regarding eligibility and final judgment shall rest with the Organizing Committee.
  • The format and rules of events are subject to change before the start of the event.

  • Network with industry leaders and potential employers
