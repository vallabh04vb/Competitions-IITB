Description:

The Socio-Exhibition Competition, integrating the concept of "Best out of Waste," is an innovative and eco-conscious event. This competition invites students to showcase their creativity and environmental awareness by crafting models related to themes such as sustainable living, renewable energy, biodiversity conservation, and more. Participants bring their projects to IIT Bombay for exhibition, transforming recycled materials into insightful and inspiring exhibits.

Structure:

  • Project Themes: Participants choose themes like sustainable living, renewable energy, biodiversity conservation, etc., guiding the creation of their projects.
  • Exhibition at IITBombay:Teams bring their models to IITBomaby for the exhibition, allowing for the vibrant display of the innovative ideas.
  • Judging createria: Projects are eveluated based on the ativite and inovation,relevnece to the theme and effective use of the recycled materials,promoting the sustainibiity aand the resourcesfulness.

    • Rules & Regulations:

  • Each team comprises up to 5 students from the same school.
  • Schools can present a maximum of 2 projects.
  • Projects must align with the chosen themes and demonstrate creative use of recycled materials.
  • Fair play and sportsmanship are expected from all participants.
  • Projects should be set up within the provided timeframe on the exhibition day.

    • Prizes & Perks:

  • Cash Prizes
    • 1st Prize - 5000
    • 2nd Prize - 3000
    • 3rd Prize - 2000
  • Abhyuday, IIT Bombay Certificates: Awarded to all participants, recognizing their efforts and creativity.
  • Exclusive Merchandise of Social Fest'24, IIT Bombay to the winners.
