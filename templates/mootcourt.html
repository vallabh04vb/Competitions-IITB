Description:

Are you ready to step into the shoes of a legal advocate and argue before a panel of esteemed judges? The Moot Court Competition 2023 invites law students from around the world to participate in a thrilling legal battle. The Moot Court Competition 2023 is a prestigious event that allows law students to showcase their advocacy, research, and argumentation skills. This year's competition will focus on a complex and contemporary legal issue, providing participants with an opportunity to engage in intellectual discourse and hone their legal expertise.

Structure:

  • Participation: In Teams of 3
  • Rounds: Preliminary Elimination (Offline), Finals (Offline)
  • Deadline for Registration: 7th January, 2023 11:59 PM

    • Rules & Regulations:

  • The competition is open to all full-time students of any under-graduation, degree, or post-graduation courses, working and learning professionals pan India.
  • Team size should be at most 3 members.
  • Preliminary elimination and finals are conducted offline at the IIT Bombay campus.
  • Teams must adhere to the specified time limits for answering questions.
  • Sportsmanship and fair play are expected from all participants.
  • The decision of the organizing team is final and binding.

    • Prizes & Perks:

  • Exclusive Merchandise to the winners & Official Certificates and LORs from Abhyuday, IIT Bombay to all the participants.
  • Network with industry leaders and potential employers
  • Attend exclusive workshops and panel discussions.
