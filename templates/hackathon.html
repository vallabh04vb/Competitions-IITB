Description:

The Hackathon Competition 2024 is a prestigious event that offers tech enthusiasts an opportunity to solve real-world problems using innovative coding solutions. This year's competition will feature a challenging problem statement that participants must address during the rounds.

Structure:

  • Participation: Individual or Team
  • Team Formation is a compulsory process. A Team needs to have a minimum of 3 candidates and a maximum of 4. If you register as an individual, we will help you be part of a team.
  • Theme of Hackathon: Social Impact. Participants will choose their own problem statement based on the theme.
  • Hackathon will be of 12 Hours divided into 7 Hours of Coding Time, 2 Hours Masterclass on DSA, and 3 hours of Screening & Presentation.
  • Full Agenda with Judging Criteria will be communicated to the respective teams after successful enrollment.
  • You will be needed to develop and submit your coding solution within the given timeframe.
  • Judges will evaluate submissions and select the top 10 performing teams to advance to the final round.
  • Top 10 Teams will present their solutions in person to a panel of judges.
  • The winning team will be announced at the end of the event.
  • Register and then join the WhatsApp Community

    • Rules & Regulations:

  • Eligibility: Students of all engineering domains are eligible.
  • Team Size: 1-4 including the Team leader
  • Registration Fee: Free
  • Venue: IIT Bombay Campus
  • Point of contact: Team leader and their email address.
  • Each individual can only be associated with one team.
  • Multiple registrations by a particular team or person shall lead to disqualification.
  • The decision of the organizing team shall be final in case of any discrepancy.
  • In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the organizing team shall be binding.

    • Prizes & Perks:

  • INR 10K Cash Prize to the winner.
  • Merchandises will be provided to 1st and 2nd Runner Up.
  • PPI (pre-placement interview) and internship opportunities for the finalists.
  • YourEngineer Courses & Training Program Discount coupons worth 50% for each participant.
  • Participation e-certificates shall be awarded to all the participants.
